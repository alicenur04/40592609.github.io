Dear Evan Hansen

Dear Evan Hansen" is a Tony Award-winning musical that follows the story of Evan Hansen, a high school student with social anxiety who gets caught up in a web of lies after the death of a classmate, Connor Murphy. Evan inadvertently becomes involved in the Murphy family's grief when a letter he wrote to himself, meant for therapy, is mistaken for Connor's suicide note. As the lies spiral out of control, Evan finds himself drawn closer to Connor's family, particularly his sister Zoe, and begins to find a sense of belonging he has always yearned for. The musical explores themes of mental health, loneliness, and the complexities of human connection, all set to a poignant and emotionally resonant score.
